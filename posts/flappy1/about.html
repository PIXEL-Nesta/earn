About the Game

Flappy — EarnYou is a modern, browser-based casual game built for fun, focus, and rewards. Inspired by the legendary Flappy Bird, this version adds a creative twist: as you play, you earn coins for every point you score. These coins can later be redeemed for digital rewards like Google Play gift cards — all within a sleek, modern interface that runs directly in your browser. No download, no sign-up, and no complicated setup required — just pure skill, patience, and entertainment.

The Concept

Our goal with Flappy — EarnYou is to blend gaming and earning in a fun, lightweight format. Unlike traditional reward apps that rely on watching ads or completing offers, this one lets your skill decide your reward. The longer you survive, the higher you score — and every score counts as a coin. Once you’ve gathered enough, you can use the Redeem page to convert your coins into real rewards. It’s simple, transparent, and designed entirely around you, the player.

Gameplay & Controls

Flappy — EarnYou keeps things intuitive. You can tap, click, or press the spacebar to make your bird flap. The challenge lies in timing — avoiding the randomly generated green pipes as they scroll toward you. Each successful pass gives you +1 coin and +1 score. But one wrong move and you’re back at zero! The combination of fast reaction time, patience, and hand-eye coordination makes the game addictive and rewarding at the same time.

Design & Aesthetics

The design of the game focuses on minimalism and clarity. The user interface is powered by a clean, dark-glass theme with golden highlights to give a premium yet comfortable look. The background gradients are crafted to provide depth without distraction. Rounded corners, subtle shadows, and smooth animations keep the focus on gameplay while maintaining a futuristic aesthetic. It looks equally good on laptops, desktops, and mobile browsers — a truly responsive experience built with modern web technologies (HTML5 Canvas, CSS3, and JavaScript).

Coins & Progress System

Every game session you play contributes to your local progress. All your coins, high scores, and redeem history are stored safely in your browser’s localStorage. This means you don’t need an account or an internet connection to save your progress. If you score 10 points in one game, that’s 10 coins added to your total instantly. The best score helps you track your personal record, while the games played count gives you a sense of your total practice and effort.

Redeem System (How Rewards Work)

The Redeem system is simple and fair. You can choose from three levels: 100 coins for ₹10, 200 coins for ₹20, or 500 coins for ₹50. Once you submit a redeem request, it appears as pending for 48 hours in your History page. After that time automatically expires, the system generates a unique 16-character code — your digital gift card code. It’s a simulation of a real reward process designed for educational and entertainment purposes, showing how web-based reward systems function securely and transparently.

History Tracking

The History page keeps a clean log of all your gameplay and redemption activity. You can view pending requests, check how much time remains before they are completed, and see your redeemed codes once they’re processed. This transparency helps you keep track of every coin you’ve earned and every reward you’ve claimed, all within a single interface. You can even clear your history anytime if you want a fresh start.

Technology Behind the Game

Flappy — EarnYou is built entirely with open web technologies — HTML5 Canvas for rendering graphics, JavaScript for game logic, and CSS3 for modern responsive design. The game runs smoothly in almost every modern browser including Chrome, Edge, and Firefox. It uses requestAnimationFrame() for optimized animations and physics updates, ensuring minimal CPU usage and maximum frame rate even on low-end devices. The entire experience is offline-first, with no external API calls or network dependencies. Every element you see, from coins to codes, runs locally in your browser for privacy and performance.

Performance & Compatibility

We optimized the gameplay for smooth performance even on older laptops and mobile devices. The graphics are lightweight, with no heavy textures or large asset files. Controls are touch-friendly, so it feels natural on both desktop and touchscreen devices. Whether you’re playing on Windows, macOS, Android, or iOS browsers, the experience remains consistent — responsive, fluid, and lag-free.

Philosophy & Mission

EarnYou was created to show that gaming doesn’t always have to be unproductive. A simple casual game can still provide a sense of growth, learning, and even reward. It’s about balancing fun with focus — challenging your reflexes while giving you something tangible to work toward. The idea came from the growing demand for fair, skill-based online activities that reward effort without manipulation or endless ads. We believe every gamer, whether casual or competitive, deserves to be appreciated for their time.

Safety & Privacy

One of the best parts of Flappy — EarnYou is that it doesn’t collect personal data. Everything happens locally in your browser. Your game progress, coins, and redeem history are all stored in your own device’s memory. No signup, no tracking, no hidden analytics — just pure fun. This ensures total safety, privacy, and control for players who just want to relax and play without worries.
