1. Amazon Mechanical Turk (MTurk)

MTurk is the world’s largest micro-task platform. You earn by doing HITs like image tagging, writing, data checking, and surveys.

Average Daily Earnings: $5 – $40 Payout: Daily

2. Clickworker

One of the easiest micro-task platforms. Work includes:

  • AI training tasks
  • Image annotation
  • Product testing
  • Text proofreading

3. Remotasks

Remotasks is perfect for users who prefer AI training tasks such as LIDAR, segmentation, bounding box, and categorization.

4. Appen

Appen provides long-term projects. The best part is flexible timing and high-paying tasks.

5. UHRS (Universal Human Relevance System)

UHRS tasks include rating, relevance checking, and AI training assistance.

You need an outsourcing company login such as OneForma or Datamime.

6. Swagbucks

A popular micro-task website that offers earnings for surveys, offers, videos, and games.

Earnings: $5–$20 per day Payout Method: PayPal, Gift Cards

7. Freecash (Fastest Growing 2025)

Freecash is considered one of the fastest-paying offerwall and task websites in 2025. Tasks include app installs, surveys, and contests.
